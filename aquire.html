How to buy


You must make sure a dog breeder has the correct papers to be allowed to sell dogs. From a breeder you will pay $3500,medical cost $1,000 for the first year then $500 after, dog items about $500, dog school(optional) $250, registration fees $58 for non fertile and $210 for a fertile dog, pet insurance $840 a year and can be higher or lower depending on the breed. Some pet stores have injured or sick dogs which will be costly depending on the injury or sickness. If you buy a puppy you will have to pay for it's vaccination and physical examinations.

how to adopt


If you adopt a dog you are saving it's life because in 5 to 7 days the will be euthanized. Any adopted dog would have their health examined, any vaccines they are missing, some may be sterilized depending on age, groomed, their behavior will be tested if they are safe to be adopted. You must make sure to get any medical documentation from the shelter.
The dog might not be fertile due to it being spayed or neutered unless specifically asked for it not to be. Adoption fees $25-$500 can be higher if it's a younger dog and you can have no adoption fees if it's a senior dog, regestration is $29, insurance depends on policy, dog breed, and age
